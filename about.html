Origin Story>

Here at H-Thai-ML, we believe that anyone and everyone should be able to enjoy authentic Thai cooking, no matter where you are, no matter where you're from.


So, we've opened the world's first interplanetary, interdimensional restaurant to serve this goal. Here, we not only serve humans, but also martians and unfathomable demons from beyond human comprehension, in our pursuit of sharing the joys of Thai cuisine with not just our world, but worlds beyond!


Enjoy our authentic, traditional meals today at one of our many locations above!
